About ImgKee

imgKee addresses the problem of password security in a previously unimaginable manner, substituting a picture in place of a text password. The picture is converted to binary on our backend then hashed and stored as a password would be, making anything secured by imgKee virtually impervious to bruteforce dictionary attacks and the like.
